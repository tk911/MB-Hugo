There be a roadMap soon™ that will show new stuff i am Working on. </p>

<p>EMA Has Cut Down on the 3rd party Libs its uses and is trying to use tons of new API blizzard has added over the years making a lot of this code unless and was nerver updated and that has made it harder to support going foreword.  </p>
